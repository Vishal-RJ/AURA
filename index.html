The Challenge

Why Telecom Churn is a Crisis

The telecom industry bleeds customers silently — traditional systems detect churn only after the damage is done.

30%
Annual churn rate faced by telecom companies due to aggressive pricing and network fluctuations. Every lost customer represents a cascading revenue failure.

⏰ Reactive Post-Mortem

Traditional systems identify churn after the SIM is deactivated, making recovery impossible.

🎯 Lack of Personalization

Generic "one-size-fits-all" offers fail to address individual customer pain points.

📉 Revenue Erosion

Acquiring a new customer is 5× more expensive than retaining an existing one.

What We Set Out To Do

Project Objectives

🔍

Early Detection

Predict high-risk customers at least 30 days before they decide to leave, enabling proactive intervention.

🔬

Granular Analysis

Isolate technical (network), financial (billing), or competitive (offers) churn drivers per customer.

⚡

Automated Intervention

Close the loop between data insights and marketing actions without human delay.

💎

Customer Lifetime Value

Maximize long-term revenue by improving brand loyalty and customer satisfaction scores.

Our Answer

Proposed Solution

A.U.R.A. is not just a predictor — it's a full prescriptive retention ecosystem powered by in-house AI.

Core

In-House Proprietary AI

A custom ML core + a domain-specific Small Language Model (SLM) — no 3rd party dependency.

Strategy

Prescriptive Retention

Not just "who" will leave, but an AI-generated personalized strategy for "how" to retain them.

Unified

A platform that ingests live CDR (Call Detail Records) and generates retention actions.

Input Intelligence

Dataset & Features

📶

Service Quality

Monitors call drop rates, 5G latency, and signal strength in the user's primary location to identify network-driven churn.

🧭

Behavioral Data

Interaction with customer support and frequency of "Plan Expiry" reminders ignored — revealing dissatisfaction patterns.

🌐

External Factors

Compares local competitor signal coverage and relative pricing of rival service packs in real-time.

📊

Usage Metrics

Analyzes daily data volume, call duration, and roaming frequency to identify sudden usage drops.

Prediction Engine

Custom Machine Learning Model

A multi-stacked ensemble optimized for imbalanced telecom datasets with continuous self-improvement.

Multi-Stacked Ensemble: XGBoost + Random Forest optimized for class imbalance in telecom churn datasets.

Custom Transformer: Processes 50+ time-series features like "latency spikes" and "recharge gaps."

SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Over-sampling) + RFE (Recursive Feature Elimination) to maximize prediction accuracy.

Weekly Feedback Loops: The model retrains automatically to adapt to changing market trends.

85%
Prediction Accuracy

Achieved through SMOTE resampling and ensemble stacking on real-world telecom CDR data.

Transparency Layer

Explainable AI & The Reasoning Engine

Eliminating the AI "Black Box" — every prediction comes with a human-readable explanation of why a customer will churn.

Custom SHAP Integration

Maps high-dimensional ML outputs into human-readable "Churn Drivers" visible to human agents.

Local vs. Global Insights

Explains why a specific user is leaving (Local) while identifying network-wide failures (Global).

Diagnostic Transparency

Shows the exact correlation between service drops and churn risk score.

The Reasoning Layer
